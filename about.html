About USB Group

Since 2000, USB Group Organisation has evolved from a single vision into a multi-sector powerhouse, delivering excellence across diverse industries with unwavering commitment to quality and innovation.

Our Journey

Founded in 2000 by visionary entrepreneur Uma Shankar Bhardwaj, USB Group began with a simple yet powerful mission: to create sustainable value across multiple sectors while maintaining the highest standards of trust and innovation. Over 25 years, we have grown from a single entity into a comprehensive business ecosystem spanning real estate, technology, finance, and beyond.

Today, with major projects worth across strategic locations including Delhi NCR, Mathura, Vrindavan, Jewar Airport, Meerut, and Ayodhya, we continue to shape India's growth story through strategic investments and innovative solutions.
