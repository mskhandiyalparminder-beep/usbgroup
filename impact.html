Our Social Initiatives

USB Group has been serving society through education, healthcare, rural upliftment, and sustainable development for over two decades.

Education

Through Samarth Charitable Trust (since 2003), we support education in slum areas and empower children with learning opportunities.
With Manav Sankalp Welfare Society, we are contributing to the development of Radheyshyam Sharma Studies & Research University in Aligarh, UP.

Health

Samarth Charitable Trust extends cancer support aid and medical help to underprivileged families.
Shree Keshav Madhav Trust (2025) is focused on establishing medical shelters, education facilities, and Ashram stays in Mathura, Vrindavan & Delhi.

Community Development

Manav Sankalp Welfare Society (FCRA No. 231661622) provides sports, legal & social services for the needy.
Haribrahma Masandgarhia Society (2017, Mathura) organizes food service initiatives to support local communities.

Sustainability & Cow Welfare

Samarth Charitable Trust also works extensively in Cow Saving & Welfare, promoting sustainable practices and compassion towards animals.
