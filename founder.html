Professional Background

With over two and a half decades of entrepreneurial excellence, Uma Shankar Bhardwaj, the visionary founder of USB Group, has built a multi-sector organization driven by innovation, integrity, and purpose. His leadership philosophy of “Growth with Responsibility” continues to guide the Group’s diverse ventures across real estate, technology, investment advisory, finance, renewable energy, and consultancy.

Under his guidance, USB Group has grown into a dynamic ecosystem that not only drives business success but also fosters social progress. Through sustained efforts in education, food distribution, blood donation drives, cow preservation, and the development of shelter homes and ashrams, he has ensured that community development remains at the heart of USB Group’s mission.
