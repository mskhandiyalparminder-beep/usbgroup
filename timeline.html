Our Journey

25 years of excellence, innovation, and growth across multiple sectors

2000 Delhi NCR

Foundation Established

Foundation Established USB Group founded by Uma Shankar Bhardwaj with a vision to build a multi-sector enterprise rooted in trust, innovation, and community impact.

2005 Mathura

Real Estate Expansion

Launched the first major residential development project, marking the beginning of a strong presence in premium real estate.

2010 Delhi NCR

IT & Software Division

Set up a dedicated technology arm focusing on IT services, software development, and digital transformation solutions.

2015 Vrindavan

Multi-Sector Growth

Expanded into finance, consultancy, and business advisory, strengthening the group as a comprehensive service provider.

2020 Jewar Airport

Major Infrastructure Projects

Initiated significant infrastructure and township developments, including projects near Jewar Airport and the Mathura-Vrindavan belt.

2025 Meerut

Renewable Energy Initiative

Launched USB Sudha Consultancy with a focus on sustainable energy solutions such as bio-CBG and green innovation.

2025(ahead) Ayodhya

Future Vision

Planning and development of upcoming mega real estate and infrastructure projects, along with expansion into new renewable energy sectors.

Looking Ahead: 2025 & Beyond

As we continue our journey of excellence, USB Group is poised for transformative growth with upcoming mega projects in Ayodhya and new ventures in renewable energy. Our commitment to innovation, quality, and social impact remains unwavering as we shape the future of Indian business.
