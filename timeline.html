Our Journey

25 years of innovation, growth, and impact across multiple sectors

2000 Delhi NCR

Foundation & IT Development

USB Group was founded by Uma Shankar Bhardwaj, focusing on IT development and Web 2.0 promotion. This marked the beginning of a technology-driven era built on trust, innovation, and digital progress.

2005 Gurgaon & Bahadurgarh

Real Estate Expansion

USB Group entered the real estate sector through projects under HUDA (Haryana Urban Development Authority). The company sold residential plots and collaborated with DLF and other leading developers, marking its strong entry into construction and real estate.

2007 Delhi

Electric Mobility & Retail Ventures

Ventured into the electric vehicle industry by opening six electric scooter showrooms across Delhi. Simultaneously launched Taghills, a multi-brand garments and retail chain, reflecting the group’s diversification and consumer-centric innovation.

2014 Mathura

Consultancy & Real Estate Services

Established NK Group (Sanskar City) and USB Consultancy, expanding into plotting, construction, and advisory. This marked the start of USB Group’s integrated growth as a diversified business enterprise.

2017 Jewar & Palwal

Farmhouses & Commercial Development

Launched residential, farmhouse, and commercial projects near the Noida Expressway in Jewar and Palwal. These developments strengthened USB Group’s presence in the premium and emerging real estate markets of UP and Haryana.

2018 Delhi NCR

Financial Market Research

Initiated work in the Indian stock market and developed in-depth analysis for future investment strategies. This laid the foundation for Gifftai, a platform designed for financial innovation and portfolio management.

2025 Mathura & Ayodhya

Launch of Gifftai & Social Initiatives

Launched Gifftai, expanding into finance, technology, and project management. Initiated large-scale real estate projects in Mathura and Ayodhya, alongside social development efforts in education, healthcare, and community welfare.

Future Ayodhya, Mathura, Aligarh

Vision for the Future

USB Group aims to lead with innovation in real estate, renewable energy, and technology. The future vision includes education initiatives, hospitals, shelter homes, sports colleges, food drives, and sustainability programs across North India.

Looking Ahead: 2025 & Beyond

USB Group continues its journey toward sustainable growth, community upliftment, and innovation. With upcoming projects in Ayodhya and nationwide social impact programs, we are shaping a stronger, greener, and more inclusive future for India.
